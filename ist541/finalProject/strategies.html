Interactive Classroom Setup

Flexible Seating

Providing flexible seating options helps students choose what works best for their learning style and comfort. Examples include bean bags, standing desks, or traditional chairs.

Technology Station

Technology stations can offer students access to tablets, computers, and assistive technology, providing multiple means of engagement and representation.

Hands-on Learning Area

Hands-on learning allows students to engage with materials physically, providing an opportunity to learn by doing. This is particularly beneficial for kinesthetic learners.
